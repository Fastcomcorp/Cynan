# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

The following versions of **Cynan IMS Core** are currently supported with security updates:

| Version | Supported          |
| ------- | ------------------ |
| 0.8.x   | ✅ Yes             |
| < 0.8.x | ❌ No              |

## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of our people-centered telecommunications infrastructure seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability within Cynan, we appreciate your help in disclosing it to us in a responsible manner.

### Reporting Process

1.  **Do not** open a public issue.
2.  Email your findings to **security@fastcomcorp.com**.
3.  Include a detailed description of the vulnerability, steps to reproduce, and any potential impact.

### Our Commitment

*   We will acknowledge receipt of your report within **48 business hours**.
*   We will provide a preliminary evaluation of the issue.
*   We will keep you informed of our progress as we work on a remediation.
*   Once fixed, we will coordinate a public disclosure with you.

### Professional Responsible Disclosure

We encourage researchers to follow industry-standard responsible disclosure practices. We do not currently offer a bug bounty program, but we provide public recognition for researchers who help us secure our infrastructure (with their permission).
